SPRNCHS’s mission is to create a global community of learners, making our world sustainable.

HISTORY

- 1970

Established as Laguna Resettlement Community High School, United Bayanihan, San Pedro, Laguna Principal : Mrs. Bartolomea E. Pedron

- 1972

Converted to SAN PEDRO RELOCATION CENTER NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, a nationally funded high school thru Republic Act 6026 Principal : Mrs. Lourdes Z. Javier

- Acquisition of lots

May 1981 – July 1, 1999, 5.7 hectares at Langgam
July 1,1999 – August, 2000, 1,000 sq.m. at Cuyab

- Creation of campuses

1986 : SPRCNHS Langgam Campus
1989 : SPRCNHS Cuyab Campus 2002 – SPRCNHS Landayan Campus June 2007 SPRCNHS changed program/curriculum from Basic Secondary Education (BEC) to Strengthened Technical Vocational Education Program (STVEP)

Accomplishment

CY – 2001

Formulated the School’s Vision,Mission,Philosophy and Goals (Center of Excellence in Tech-Voc Education in the Philippines)

CY – 2003

Passed the Accreditation Program of the University of Asia and the Pacific and the Ford Foundation, in Collaboration with the DEPED thru the Project Sterling Silver ( Level – 3 )

CY – 2004

Transformed the School Curricular Program from BEC to Applied Academics for Excellence ( APEX ), First in the Philippines

CY – 2007

SPRCNHS is one of the 282 Tech Voc High Schools of DEPED to Implement the STVEP ( Transformed from Applied Academics to Tech Voc Program )
